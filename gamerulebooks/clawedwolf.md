# ClawedWolf (爪狼杀) Rulebook

## Overview

ClawedWolf is a social deduction game for **6 players**. Two hidden ClawedWolves secretly eliminate villagers each night, while the Good team tries to identify and vote them out during the day. Bluff, deduce, and survive!

## Roles

| Role | Team | Count | Ability |
|------|------|-------|---------|
| **ClawedWolf** (爪狼) | Evil | 2 | Vote with your partner each night to kill one player. You know who the other wolf is. |
| **Seer** (预言家) | Good | 1 | Investigate one player each night to learn if they are Good or Evil. |
| **Guard** (守卫) | Good | 1 | Protect one player each night from being killed. Cannot protect the same player two nights in a row. |
| **Villager** (平民) | Good | 2 | No special ability — use your wits to find the wolves. |

## Phase Flow

Each round follows this sequence:

### Night Phase

1. **ClawedWolves** — The two wolves secretly choose a player to kill.
2. **Seer** — The Seer secretly investigates one player's alignment (Good or Evil).
3. **Guard** — The Guard secretly chooses one player to protect from the wolves' kill.

### Day Phase

4. **Announce** — The results of the night are revealed (who was killed, or if the Guard saved someone).
5. **Discuss** — Each living player speaks once, in seat order. Share suspicions, claim roles, or bluff.
6. **Vote** — Each living player votes to eliminate one player (or abstains). Majority wins; ties result in no elimination.
7. **Result** — The voted player is eliminated (if any).

After the day result, the game checks for a winner. If no one has won yet, a new night begins.

## Win Conditions

- **Good team wins** — All ClawedWolves are eliminated.
- **Evil team wins** — Living ClawedWolves equal or outnumber living Good players.

## Strategy Tips

- **ClawedWolves**: Coordinate kills to take out the Seer or Guard early. Blend in during discussions and avoid drawing suspicion.
- **Seer**: Investigate the most suspicious players. Share your findings carefully — the wolves will target you if you reveal yourself too soon.
- **Guard**: Protect whoever you think the wolves will target, especially the Seer if their identity is known.
- **Villagers**: Listen closely to speeches and note inconsistencies. Vote based on behavior and claims.
